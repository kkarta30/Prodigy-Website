About our team

Prodigy was created by four first year Creative Media & Game Technologies students. We were given the assignment to create an arcade like game using these three elements: Sensation, Build & Monsters. During the brainstorm period we combined all of our best ideas and settled on a tower defense game. Prodigy was created. We thought that a tower defense game would be a bit out of the ordinary, & also really fun to make. Later we figured out that building a game as such was not as straight forward as we had anticipated and came across lots of bumps. This turned out to be a valuable learning experience for working on such a project.

.

Bas & Fernando were responsible for the development of the game. While Bas was the leading programmer, Fernando took more of a supporting role. Most visual design in the game was done by Donovan, while Kevin worked on the logo & the website's design. What was most valuable about this process, was our ability to work as a team, and discuss different possibilities and opportunities on the fly. Great communication, planning and motivating each other helped us push this project to what it has become!
